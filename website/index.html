YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

WHAT IS THE THINK OF IT ?YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

YLP exists for the youth to act in unison with an aim to create an impact by addressing social, political, and environmental issues. It makes them; the youth realize the significance of a youth-led nation. They need to lead life by their inherent nature; not by the conventional means, which this program supports and remains at disposal for. It’s rather easy to rely upon secondary sources such as newspapers and news channels to keep a tab on everything happening around the globe however gone are the days when these sources were devoid of bias and prejudice. It’s time for the youth to organize themselves amidst this cut-throat quest for truth which is never easy to discover let alone expressed in coherent terms. The program blends learning with leadership and fun, aiming at the wholesome development of the most important and energetic cluster of our society – the youth!

To answer the ‘What’ behind this program’s existence would be to usher the youth to, in very straightforward terms, ‘Think of It’ i.e. the ongoing issues pertaining to multifarious spheres, morality, ideals, the sense of justice that prevails and to conjecture upon ideas that should be prevailing instead. Youth and leadership share an intrinsic yet undervalued relationship, we are here to bridge the gap between the two and provide a platform to equip the leaders of tomorrow.

Youth Leadership

The Youth Leadership Program is about equipping you with the skill set required to be a leader while ensuring your growth.

You will get

You will get a chance to collaborate with the young leaders of our nation. We believe that you are capable of being mature and understanding and that leadership doesn’t necessarily have much to do with age.

COURSE

During the course of your involvement in THINK OF IT – Youth Leadership Program, you’ll be involved in various activities related to your city chapters.

Activities

These activities are not limited to conferences, programs/ discussions on policy making and problem solving which involves working with the local politicians, officials involved and TOI – City Chapter VPs and Managers.

Emphasis

Our major emphasis rests on hands-on experience and practical knowledge rather than merely being a platform for dialogue. The time has come to uphold actions over words..

Course of the Program

During the course of your involvement in THINK OF IT – Youth Leadership Program, you’ll be involved in various activities related to your city chapters. These activities are not limited to conferences, programs/ discussions on policy making and problem solving which involves working with the local politicians, officials involved and TOI – City Chapter VPs and Managers.

We encourage youth to be a part of the summits representing them like The Youth Parliament and The State Conferences hosted bi-annually to bring the leader within them in front of eminent persona’s whose experiences offer a lot to learn about politics, governance and the world.

There are city chapters which the youth can partake in after registering and discuss matters related to their cities. The promotions and other managerial positions will be filled on the merit of performance.
